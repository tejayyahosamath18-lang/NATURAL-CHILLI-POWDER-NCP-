Project Description – Natural Chilli Powder (NCP) Business Analysis & Marketing Strategy
This project focuses on the analysis, marketing, and business development of Natural Chilli Powder (NCP) and its bottled product range. The objective was to evaluate market opportunities, identify target customers, analyze product benefits, develop pricing and marketing strategies, and create data-driven business insights for better decision-making. The project combines business intelligence, financial planning, and product marketing to support business growth.
